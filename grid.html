A little about our work

Ahh, the good ol' annual family photos. Good thing about today's day and age is that you can pick a photographer to run around outside in your backyard if you want, the dogs are a definite MUST, AND YOU DON'T HAVE TO GO TO A STUDIO AT THE MALL! YAY!

The reason my business exists today: seniors graduating high school! I first started getting hired to take my friend's senior photos when I was only 16 years old, and I have never stopped loving it. I can't wait to hear about all your life plans as you get ready to GRADUATE high school!

Whether you want your love story to be captured from the inside of a beautiful studio, wandering through a forest, at the top of a mountain, or just hanging out at home, I'm here for it. Ask me about my wedding guide!
